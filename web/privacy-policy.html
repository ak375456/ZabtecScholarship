Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZABTEC Enterprise collects, uses, stores, and shares information when students, administrators, HEC reviewers, or other authorized users use the ZABTEC scholarship platform, website, mobile application, and related services.

Who we serve

The platform is intended for Pakistani students and for students studying in or applying through universities recognized or registered by the Higher Education Commission (HEC), Pakistan. Scholarships and related services may be connected with HEC, universities, partner organizations, or other scholarship providers.

Information we collect

We collect information needed to register users, verify eligibility, process applications, and manage scholarship services. This may include:

  • Account information such as full name, CNIC, phone number, email address, password, role, and login details.
  • Personal profile information such as date of birth, gender, domicile, address, city, province, district, and contact details.
  • Family information such as parent or guardian names, CNIC numbers, contact details, occupation, income, and household information.
  • Education information such as institution, board, program or group, marks, grades, percentages, degree status, and academic records.
  • Documents and images uploaded by the user, including CNIC images, educational documents, certificates, photos, PDFs, and supporting evidence.
  • Application data such as progress, submission status, review notes, approval or rejection status, and scholarship matching details.
  • Payment or challan information such as challan number, due date, payment status, amount, and receipt records.
  • Technical information such as device type, browser, IP address, app version, crash logs, access times, and security logs.
  • Messages, support requests, feedback, or other communications sent to us.

Why we collect this data

We use collected information to:

  • Create and secure student, administrator, and HEC reviewer accounts.
  • Verify student identity, CNIC details, educational background, and eligibility.
  • Prepare, submit, review, approve, reject, or track scholarship applications.
  • Connect eligible students with HEC, university, partner, or future scholarship opportunities.
  • Generate registration fee challans, receipts, and related records.
  • Review uploaded documents and detect incomplete, false, duplicate, or suspicious applications.
  • Provide support, send service notices, and communicate important updates.
  • Improve platform performance, security, reliability, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal, audit, fraud prevention, scholarship administration, and institutional record requirements.

How we share information

We may share relevant information with HEC, universities, scholarship providers, banks, payment partners, service providers, hosting providers, auditors, legal authorities, and authorized platform staff when needed to operate the service, verify eligibility, process applications, investigate misuse, or comply with official requirements.

We do not sell student personal information. We only share information for platform operation, scholarship administration, verification, security, compliance, or with the user's consent.

Scholarship availability

Scholarships and services connected with HEC, universities, or other providers can be changed, delayed, paused, or cancelled at any time by the relevant authority or provider. ZABTEC Enterprise does not guarantee that a scholarship will remain available, that an application will be selected, or that funds, approvals, or schedules will not change.

Data accuracy

Users are responsible for providing accurate, complete, and current information. Incorrect CNIC details, academic records, documents, income information, or contact details may delay review or result in rejection, suspension, or cancellation of an application or service.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide services, manage applications, maintain scholarship records, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, comply with legal or institutional obligations, and support audit requirements. Some records may be retained even after an account is closed where necessary for verification, compliance, or security.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. However, no online service, mobile application, server, or data transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should keep passwords confidential and immediately report unauthorized account activity.

User choices and requests

Users may request correction of inaccurate account or application information through the support or contact options provided in the platform. Some information cannot be changed after submission if it is needed for verification, audit, fraud prevention, or official scholarship records.

Children and guardians

Students who are below the age of legal consent should use the platform with the permission and guidance of a parent or legal guardian.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when services, scholarship programs, technology, legal requirements, or operational needs change. The updated policy will apply from the date it is posted.

Contact

For privacy questions, corrections, or account support, contact ZABTEC Enterprise through the official contact details provided in the app or on the website.
